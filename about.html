About Us

Hello! Welcome to Sandy's Petshop! We have been in business for ten years and still going strong! We love our community and are strong advocates for animals. Our staff are highly trained and are all huge animal lovers! We would love to help you take care of your pets! We specialize in dog, cat, and reptile supplies. We have a large selection of food, treats, toys, dental and cleaning supplies, crates, beds, habitats, feeding supplies and more. If you are curious if we carry something please don't hesitate to call. We also offer grooming services.
